A multi-channel notification platform and community hub that tracks every major competitive and academic exam.

Official Data Integrity: Every listing must include a direct link to the official notification and the original PDF for transparency and trust.

Peer Communities: Dedicated digital spaces for like-minded aspirants appearing for the same exams to share resources and support.

Simplified UX: A website and alert system designed for high 'understandability,' specifically tailored to help first-generation learners navigate the application process.

The Goal:
